What to Expect from Your Appointment

During the visit, our technician will inspect your location to make sure we can get a hot tub installed for you. Our technician will:

  • Measure passageways for clearance and accessibility
  • Inspect decking and flooring for adequate structural support
  • Evaluate electrical requirements
  • Discuss cost-saving ideas on the location and design of your hot tub
  • Offer safety suggestions on the installation and usage
  • Provide a written report that will assist in shopping, regardless of which hot tub you eventually choose

To make sure we get the right hot tub selected, it is critical that all homeowners and individuals involved in the customization process be present for the entire visit. Our technician will be there for about 60 minutes to answer any questions you may have.
